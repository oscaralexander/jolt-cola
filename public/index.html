America’s #1 hacker drink is back

Launched in 1985 as America’s first carbonated energy drink, Jolt Cola soon won the hearts of hackers, gamers and other night owls across the US. Despite featuring in several Hollywood films, a European introduction never came before the company behind Jolt went bankrupt in 2009.

In 2017 the cult drink returned under new management, sporting its original design and flavor. A new chance to grab the cola that fueled all-nighters in the pioneering days of the web.
